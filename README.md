# Retail-sales-and-profit-analysis-dashboard
1.   Project title:
Sales and Profit Analysis Dashboard

2.  Short Description:
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify business trends, profit distribution, and product performance. The interactive Power BI dashboard helps visualize key metrics such as total sales, profit, and order count. It enables businesses to understand which categories, sub-categories, and regions contribute most to revenue and profitability.

3.   Tech stack:
   Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
   Excel / CSV – Data source
   Data Cleaning & Transformation – Power Query
   DAX – Calculations and measures
   Data Visualization – Charts, filters, and KPIs

5.Key Business Questions Answered:

This dashboard answers important business intelligence questions:
Sales Performance
1️.Which sub-categories generate the highest sales?
2️.Which product categories dominate total sales?
Profitability
3️.Which sub-categories generate the highest profit?
4️.Which states or regions are most profitable?
Logistics
5️.Which shipping mode is used most frequently?
6️.How does shipping choice affect sales distribution?
Operations
7️.Are there high sales but low profit products?
8️.Which products should be promoted or discontinued?


6.Key Insights From the Dashboard:
Based on the visualization:

Sales Insights
Chairs and Bookcases generate the highest sales
Labels and Fasteners contribute the least sales

Profit Insights
Bookcases produce the highest profit
Some categories show moderate sales but lower profit margins

Regional Insights
England generates the highest profit
Other regions contribute significantly less

Shipping Insights
Economy shipping dominates (~59%)
Immediate shipping has the lowest usage

 Category Insights
Sales distribution:
Furniture ≈ 45%
Office Supplies ≈ 33%
Technology ≈ 22%
This means Furniture is the core revenue driver.
